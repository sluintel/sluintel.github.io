What Is a Free Agent & Why It Matters: The John Stones Free Agent Scenario

In football, a "free agent" is a player whose contract with their current club has expired, making them eligible to sign with any other team without a transfer fee. The Anatomy of a Bosman Move: What It Means for John Stones

The term "Bosman ruling" underpins the modern concept of free agency in European football. Instituted in 1995, it allows professional football players in the European Union to move to another club for free at the end of their contract. For a player like John Stones, this means that as his current deal with Manchester City approaches its conclusion, his market value shifts dramatically, not in what a club pays for him, but in what they offer him.

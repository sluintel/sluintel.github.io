  • Refresh Your Browser/App: Sometimes a simple page refresh (`Ctrl+R` or `Cmd+R`) or restarting the Claude app can resolve temporary glitches.

  • Clear Browser Cache & Cookies: Corrupted browser data can interfere with web applications. Clear your cache and cookies, then try accessing Claude again.
  • Try a Different Browser or Device: If one browser or device isn't working, switch to another. This helps isolate if the problem is specific to your setup.

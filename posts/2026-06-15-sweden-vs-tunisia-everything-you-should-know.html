Sweden's economy thrives on innovation, strong export sectors (engineering, IT), and a comprehensive welfare system. Its regulated labor market prioritizes social safety nets and work-life balance, yielding low unemployment and minimal income inequality.

Tunisia's economy, diversified across agriculture, mining, manufacturing, and tourism, faces challenges common to emerging markets. Higher youth unemployment and efforts to attract foreign investment persist. Post-2011 reforms aim for liberalization, but progress remains uneven.

Cultural Fabric: Secularism vs. Islamic Heritage

Swedish culture is often egalitarian, individualistic, and highly secular. Values like consensus and efficiency are paramount. Religious practice is low, and gender equality is deeply embedded.

Essential Tools and Platforms for AI Engineers

To effectively build and deploy AI solutions, Tata Consultancy Services AI engineers rely on a powerful toolkit. Here are some of the most critical ones you'll encounter and use regularly:

  • MLflow: An open-source platform for managing the end-to-end machine learning lifecycle, including experimentation, reproducibility, and deployment.

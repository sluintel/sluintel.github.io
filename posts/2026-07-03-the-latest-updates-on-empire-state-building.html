Mastering Your Visit: How to Experience the New Empire State Building

With so many exciting changes, how do you ensure you get the most out of your visit to this iconic NYC landmark? It's all about planning smart and embracing the new digital tools available.

  1. Book Your Tickets in Advance (Seriously): This is non-negotiable. Pre-booking online saves you significant time waiting in ticket lines, especially during peak seasons. Choose a specific time slot to minimize your on-site wait.
  2. Download the Official ESB App: Before you even arrive, get the Empire State Building official app on your smartphone.

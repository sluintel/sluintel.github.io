Pro Tips for Post-Breach Security

Just surviving a data leak isn't enough; thriving in a post-breach world means adopting a proactive and slightly paranoid mindset. Here's what the experts recommend:

  • Freeze Your Credit: This is a powerful step. A credit freeze prevents new creditors from accessing your report, making it incredibly difficult for identity thieves to open new accounts in your name. You can temporarily lift it when you need to apply for credit.
